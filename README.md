# Enterprise AI Blueprint Series (EABS)

*Reference Architectures, Frameworks and Playbooks for Enterprise AI*

A cohesive series that equips enterprise teams with the architectures, frameworks, and playbooks needed to design, deliver, and govern AI at scale.

## Volumes

| Volume | Title | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Enterprise Agentic SDLC](./volume1-enterprise-agentic-sdlc.md) | Reference architecture and playbook for building AI products through a governed, agentic software development lifecycle — from idea to production. |
| 2 | [Enterprise AI Skills & Scaffolding Framework](./volume2-enterprise-ai-skills-scaffolding.md) | Skills taxonomy for enterprise AI teams combined with the 8-dimensional scaffolding framework that makes AI reliable, auditable, and governable. |
| 3 | [Enterprise Repository Intelligence](./volume3-enterprise-repository-intelligence.md) | Reference architecture for AI-driven code intelligence, semantic search, and knowledge graphs across enterprise codebases and polyglot monorepos. |
| 4 | [Enterprise AI Modernization](./volume4-enterprise-ai-modernization.md) | Playbook for modernizing legacy systems and processes with AI — covering application portfolio assessment, migration patterns, and risk management. |
| 5 | [Enterprise Digital Twin](./volume5-enterprise-digital-twin.md) | Reference architecture for enterprise digital twins powered by AI — real-time simulation, predictive analytics, and AI-driven decision support. |
| 6 | [Responsible Enterprise AI](./volume6-responsible-enterprise-ai.md) | Framework for governance, ethics, fairness, transparency, explainability, and regulatory compliance across the enterprise AI portfolio. |

## How to Read This Series

1. Start with **Volume 1** to understand how AI products are built and delivered through an agentic SDLC.
2. Read **Volume 2** for the skills and scaffolding foundations that underpin every other volume.
3. Choose **Volumes 3–5** based on your enterprise focus area: code intelligence, legacy modernization, or digital twins.
4. Apply **Volume 6** as the governance and responsibility overlay across your entire AI portfolio.
5. Use the **Scaffolding Deep-Dives** as design checklists when building or scaling individual AI products.
